Light Summer: Soft and Serene

Light Summer is characterized by soft, muted tones that reflect the calmness and gentleness of summer. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Light Summer thrives on soft, cool-toned hues that are delicate and serene. Think of soft blues, lavender, and light pinks. Here are some key color categories:

Soft and Muted: These colors define the Light Summer palette. Think soft aqua, light rose, lavender blue, and soft peach.

Cool Tones: Shades like light periwinkle, cool mint, and soft greys complement the palette with their refreshing hues.

Neutrals: Light Summer balances its soft tones with light neutrals like ivory and soft taupe, avoiding harsh contrasts.

Explore these colors to create a wardrobe that embodies the tranquility and lightness of summer.

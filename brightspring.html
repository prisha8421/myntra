Bright Spring: Vivid and Warm

Bright Spring is characterized by vivid, warm colors that reflect the freshness and energy of spring. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Bright Spring thrives on warm, vibrant hues that are lively and cheerful. Think of coral, yellow, and lime green. Here are some key color categories:

Warm and Vivid: These colors are the essence of Bright Spring. Think peach, coral, and yellow.

Vibrant Tones: Shades like orange, teal, and turquoise blue complement the palette with their brightness.

Neutrals: Bright Spring can balance these intense hues with warm neutrals like cream, beige, and tan.

Experiment with these colors to create a wardrobe that exudes energy and warmth, perfect for the spring season.

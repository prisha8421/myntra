True Autumn: Warm and Earthy

True Autumn is characterized by warm, earthy tones that reflect the richness of the autumn season. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

True Autumn thrives on warm, rich hues that are grounded and organic. Think of deep browns, rich olives, and warm mustards. Here are some key color categories:

Warm and Earthy: These colors are the essence of True Autumn. Think coffee brown, mustard, rust, and olive.

Rich Tones: Shades like burgundy, maroon, and copper complement the palette with their depth.

Neutrals: True Autumn can balance these intense hues with warm neutrals like off white and soft bronze.

Experiment with these colors to create a wardrobe that exudes warmth and richness, perfect for the autumn season.

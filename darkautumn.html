Dark Autumn: Rich and Warm Tones

Dark Autumn is characterized by rich, earthy tones that echo the colors of a sunset in autumn. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Dark Autumn thrives on warm, deep hues that reflect the changing leaves and warm spices. Think of burnt orange, olive green, rich browns, and deep golds. Here are some key color categories:

Warm and Rich: These colors are the heart of Dark Autumn. Think burnt orange, rust, olive, deep teal, and warm browns.

Earthy Tones: Shades like mustard, terracotta, and deep gold add depth and richness to the palette.

Complementary Colors: Dark Autumn pairs well with warm neutrals like camel and warm beige, as well as deep, earthy greens and browns.

Experiment with these colors to find combinations that evoke the warmth and richness of autumn in your wardrobe.

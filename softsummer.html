Soft Summer: Cool and Muted

Soft Summer is characterized by cool, muted tones that reflect the gentle and soothing aspects of summer. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Soft Summer thrives on cool, muted hues that are calming and elegant. Think of soft greys, gentle lavenders, and muted teals. Here are some key color categories:

Cool and Muted: These colors define the Soft Summer palette. Think charcoal, soft lavender, mauve, and muted teal.

Subtle Tones: Shades like soft olive, sea green, and navy blue complement the palette with their subtlety.

Neutrals: Soft Summer balances its cool tones with soft neutrals like off white and soft grey, avoiding harsh contrasts.

Explore these colors to create a wardrobe that embodies the cool and muted elegance of summer.

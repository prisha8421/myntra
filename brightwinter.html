BRIGHT WINTER

Bright Winter: Bold and Vibrant

Bright Winter is characterized by clear, bold colors that have a cool undertone, reflecting the crispness of winter. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Bright Winter thrives on vivid, high-contrast hues that are cool-toned. Think of deep navy blue, bright magenta, and clear red. Here are some key color categories:

Clear and Bold: These colors are the essence of Bright Winter. Think deep navy blue, bright magenta, clear red, and bright teal.

High Contrast: Shades like silver and off white complement the palette with their crispness.

Accent Colors: Sea green adds a refreshing touch to the palette.

Experiment with these colors to create a wardrobe that exudes vibrancy and clarity, perfect for the winter season.

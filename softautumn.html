Soft Autumn: Warm and Subtle

Soft Autumn is characterized by warm, muted tones that reflect the gentle transition of autumn. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Soft Autumn thrives on warm, muted hues that are gentle and inviting. Think of soft olives, dusty roses, and warm taupes. Here are some key color categories:

Warm and Muted: These colors define the Soft Autumn palette. Think taupe, soft olive, dusty rose, and muted peach.

Earth Tones: Shades like rust, tan, and khaki complement the palette with their warmth.

Neutrals: Soft Autumn balances its warm tones with soft neutrals like cream and soft taupe, avoiding harsh contrasts.

Explore these colors to create a wardrobe that embodies the warmth and subtlety of autumn.

Hourglass Body Type

The hourglass body type is defined by a well-proportioned silhouette with a balanced bust and hips, and a defined waist. Embrace your curves with dresses that highlight your natural shape and create a streamlined look.

Showcasing Your Curves:

  • Fitted Dresses: Embrace your beautiful curves with fitted dresses that hug your figure in all the right places. Look for styles with seams that accentuate your waistline. Explore
  • Bodycon Dresses: These form-fitting dresses will show off your hourglass figure perfectly. Choose a thicker fabric like ponte knit for added structure if you prefer a more smoothing effect. Explore
  • Wrap Dresses: Wrap dresses are a versatile option for hourglass figures. The wrap style cinches your waist and creates a beautiful silhouette. Explore

Maintaining Balance:

  • Sheath Dresses: These timeless dresses with a straight silhouette are a great choice for hourglass figures. They skim your curves without being too tight, creating a sleek and elegant look. Explore
  • Pencil Skirts: Pencil skirts paired with a fitted top highlight your natural waistline and showcase your curves beautifully. Explore

Adding Interest:

  • Peplum Dresses: Peplum dresses add a touch of femininity and can visually define your waist. Explore
  • Belted Dresses: A belt cinches your waist and accentuates your hourglass figure. Look for dresses with a built-in belt or add your own. Explore

Materials:

  • Structured Fabrics: Opt for fabrics with a bit of structure like ponte knit, scuba fabric, or tailored crepe. These fabrics will hold their shape and flatter your curves.
  • Stretch Fabrics: Stretchy fabrics like jersey or ponte knit can be comfortable and flattering, especially for casual dresses. Look for styles with a good balance of structure and stretch.

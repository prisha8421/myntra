True Winter: Cool and Bold

True Winter is characterized by cool, bold colors that reflect the crisp and clear nature of winter. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

True Winter thrives on cool, striking hues that are vibrant and intense. Think of black, white, and navy blue. Here are some key color categories:

Cool and Bold: These colors are the essence of True Winter. Think black, grey, and charcoal.

Vibrant Tones: Shades like red, teal, and olive green complement the palette with their depth.

Neutrals: True Winter can balance these intense hues with neutrals like white and navy blue.

Experiment with these colors to create a wardrobe that exudes boldness and sophistication, perfect for the winter season.

Light Spring: Fresh and Delicate

Light Spring is characterized by soft, warm-toned colors that reflect the freshness and delicacy of springtime. Here's a detailed breakdown of its characteristics:

Colors:

Light Spring thrives on light and warm hues with a golden undertone. Think of fresh blossoms and gentle sunlight. Here are some key color categories:

Soft and Warm: These colors embody the essence of Light Spring. Think light peach, golden yellow, coral pink, and light aqua.

Fresh Tones: Shades like light turquoise, warm mint, and soft lavender complement the palette with their subtle vibrancy.

Neutrals: Light Spring balances these soft hues with warm, light neutrals like ivory, cream, and light taupe.

Experiment with these colors to create a wardrobe that radiates freshness and warmth, perfect for the spring season.

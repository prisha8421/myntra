Check the color of your wrist blood vessels 1-2cm from your wrist. If it is blueish-purple, you are cool toned. If it is green, you are warm toned and if you see a mix of both, you are likely to be neutral toned.

Do you look better in gold or silver jewellery? If gold, you are warm toned and if silver, cool toned. Looking good in both could mean you have a neutral hue.
